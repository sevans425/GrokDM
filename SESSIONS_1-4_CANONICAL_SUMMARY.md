    11	### Session 1: The Shadowfell Incursion
    12	Eden arrived in Thornhaven following his father's letter about a potential connection between stolen grimoires and current events. He discovered three townspeople had been kidnapped by shadow creatures through a planar rift. Working with Captain Rorick Stoneshield and Torvald Copperkettle, Eden closed the first rift at the old logging camp, rescued all three victims, and discovered a buried idol anchor was maintaining the portal. This was the cult's first ritual site.
