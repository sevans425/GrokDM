## Character Notes

**Sleep Deprivation:**
Two weeks without more than 4 hours sleep while planning operations or processing crises. Age 20 - body can survive it, judgment can't. First mandatory rest period just ordered.

**Arranged Conclusions:**
Understanding that his capabilities were deliberately built by father over 20 years, but claiming agency in how he uses them. "Arranged opportunity, not arranged conclusion."

**Certainty Over Fear:**
Resistance to Vorthanis's supernatural terror came from certainty about purpose (duty, protecting vulnerable, preventing mass casualties), not absence of fear. The Weave responds to certainty as magical state.

**Resource Discipline:**
Asks for external rubrics to prevent rationalization. Knows tendency to optimize and squeeze every advantage. Requesting guardrails before gaining power to abuse.

**Professional Development:**
- Tormund: "Third-year thinking" / "Master-level approach"
- Displays advanced tactical thinking but still massive learning curve
- 20-year preparation validating while showing how much remains unknown
