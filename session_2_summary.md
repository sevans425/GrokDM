     4	## Session Recap
     5	
     6	**The Crisis Resolved**
     7	
     8	Following the harrowing events of Session 1, where Eden Sagebough closed a Shadowfell portal and shattered a mysterious stone idol at the old logging camp, Session 2 opened with the young wizard-detective collaborating with Captain Rorick Stoneshield to prevent a catastrophic ritual. Over breakfast at The Sleeping Bear Inn—run by the warm dwarf innkeeper Greta Ironfoot—Eden and Rorick consulted with Miriam Crowsinger, the eccentric hedge witch who lived among hundreds of crows at her cottage east of Thornhaven. Miriam identified three ritual sites where planar anchors were being placed: the logging camp (already secured), ancient standing stones three miles northwest, and old watchtower ruins five miles southwest.
     9	
    10	Eden devised a daring plan: infiltrate the standing stones disguised as a cultist, steal their ritual materials, and coordinate a three-pronged strike force assault. The plan succeeded brilliantly. At midday, Eden—disguised with Miriam's help—walked into the standing stones ritual site where two cultists were preparing the second anchor under the direction of a robed figure. Using Minor Illusion and quick thinking, Eden stole the Focus Box containing a terrifying ritual dagger, Elara's journal, and blood vials, destroying the vials and confiscating a second intact anchor idol before escaping.
    11	
    12	In the afternoon, the strike force—led by Rorick and including Sergeant Brickle, Thompson (a guard with a warhammer), and other guards—assaulted the logging camp. Eden employed psychological warfare, wielding the glowing ritual dagger and claiming to be a "divine champion" sent to stop the cult. The bluff worked spectacularly. The cult leader, revealed to be **Elara Moonshadow herself**—the long-missing Master Archivist from the Royal Archives—was captured along with two other cultists. The revelation that Elara was alive, had led the death cult for twenty years, and planned an apocalyptic ritual to empower Bhaal through mass sacrifice shocked everyone.
    13	
    14	By late afternoon, the strike force secured the standing stones, capturing two more cultists and destroying the second anchor. By evening, they reached the watchtower ruins, captured a final fanatical cultist, and destroyed the third anchor. Miriam warded all three sites against future planar incursion. With all six cultists imprisoned and the ritual materials confiscated, the threat was neutralized.
    15	
    16	During her nighttime interrogation, Elara—cooperative and seemingly seeking redemption despite being possessed by a weakening Deathstalker entity—confessed everything. She revealed that the three stolen grimoires ("The Shadowfell Concordat," "Rites of the Dusk Court," and "The Book of Anchors") were hidden in a cave system at Raven's Crag, ten miles northwest of town. Her journal documented twenty years of descent into darkness and detailed planning for the ritual that would have killed everyone in Thornhaven—approximately 300 people—to create a permanent Shadowfell bridgehead.
    17	
    18	The session ended peacefully the following morning, with Eden and Miriam recovering from exhaustion at The Sleeping Bear Inn. Thornhaven was saved, the immediate crisis averted, and Eden had unknowingly prevented the very apocalypse his father, Aldrin Sagebough, had been investigating through the stolen grimoire mystery.
