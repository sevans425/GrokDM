### Contract Terms (Reconstructed)

**Sanctuary Contract between King Aldric II and Vorthanis:**

**What Vorthanis Agreed To:**
- Voluntary indefinite physical containment
- Separation of body from essence
- Protection from "the Hunt" (other exarchs hunting newly-elevated Vorthanis)

**What Vorthanis Was Promised:**
- Protection from other exarchs while he consolidated power
- Release upon "insurrection" among the exarchs (power shift/Breaking)
- Ability to resume his "original purpose" when released

**What the Crown Agreed To:**
- Maintain containment wards in perpetuity
- Keep physical form intact and preserved
- Provide sanctuary until insurrection/power shift occurred

**Critical Clause:**
*"Destruction of the physical form voids this agreement and releases all parties from obligation."*

Destroying the body = immediate release from all contract terms.

### The Redacted Sections

Someone scraped away critical text decades ago. Through careful reconstruction using Comprehend Languages and physical impression analysis, Eden and Thaddeus recovered:

**"Protection from THE OTHER EXARCHS"**
- Vorthanis was hiding from other exarchs who were hunting him
- Newly elevated exarchs are vulnerable during consolidation
- He couldn't fight them, so he hid

**"Upon INSURRECTION... shall resume... original purpose as DEATH HERALD"**
- Not "resurrection" (Bhaal's return)
- Not specific event - ANY insurrection/power shift
- Original purpose: **Death Herald**
- Facilitator of mass death events (plagues, famines, wars, disasters)
- Prepares populations for Bhaal's "harvest"

**The Timeline:**
- 1246: Vorthanis becomes exarch
- 1247: Immediately seeks imprisonment for protection
- 59 years: Waits in safety while consolidating divine power
- Present: Insurrection imminent, contract about to release him

1. King's approval (Crown authority)
2. Kethris's consent (reformist exarch participation)
3. Temple mobilization (Celeste's forces)
4. Civilian evacuation (no collateral casualties)
5. Crown protection for Eden afterward (permanent exarch target)
