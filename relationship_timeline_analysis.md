## LYREN ASHVEIL

### First Meeting (Day 9 - Thornhaven)

**Context:** Eden extracting Kethris from Vorthanis's torture chamber

**Initial Interaction:**
- Lyren was Kethris's daughter, present during extraction
- Professional/tense - focused on saving her mother
- No romantic context - crisis management

**Immediate Aftermath:**
- Lyren grateful for mother's rescue
- Eden focused on tactical success
- No personal connection established yet

### The Knife Threat (Day 12 - Staged Insurrection)

**The Fake Coup:**
- Eden held knife to Kethris's throat as part of theatrical insurrection plan
- Lyren didn't know it was theater
- **Her genuine reaction:** Shock, betrayal, tears, frozen with weapon half-drawn
- *"Eden, please—"* (crying, thinking he'd actually snapped)

**The Reveal:**
- Kethris: *"Lyren, stand down. It's theater."*
- Lyren: *"WHAT?!"*
- Realized her authentic response sold the deception to exarchs
- Saved Eden's life with healing potion (first time)
- Threw herself in front of Malthor's death burst (second time, nearly died)

**Post-Battle:**
- Angry. Processing. *"People DIED, Eden."*
- But also: *"You're welcome. For saving your life. Just don't make me regret it."*

### The Conservatory (Day 13 - First Connection)

**Morning After Funeral:**

Eden and Lyren both processing parent-dating revelation.

**Meditation Attempt:**
- Lyren tried to help Eden relax
- *"Having friends is weird when you've never had them before."*
- Shared vulnerability about both being shaped by parents' work
- Both lonely overachievers who don't know how to just exist

**The Games:**

**Dragon's Hoard** - Intense board game competition
- Eden used Portent strategically (11 to win, 3 to sabotage her)
- THREE natural 6s in final turns to win
- Lyren: *"You SABOTAGED me with divine magic!"*
- Pure competitive joy, trash talk, connection

**The Puzzle Box Torture:**
- Lyren deliberately solving incorrectly to watch Eden suffer
- His eyes started twitching
- Failed CON save, exploded with instructions
- Both laughing afterward

**The Closeness:**
- Standing near the fountain after games
- *"I'm feeling one thing and thinking another. The tension makes me want to spiral. But I won't. Because I want to stay close."*
- Lyren: *"Yeah. Me too."*

**The First Kiss:**
- Lyren took his hand. *"Is this okay?"*
- Eden tugged her closer
- *"Oh, shut your mouth."* → leaned in
- Awkward but good. Noses bumped. But really good.
- *"Well, do it again."* → So he did.

**What Changed:**
- From strangers to friends to... something more
- In one afternoon
- Pure connection through shared disaster energy

### The Garden Protocols (Day 14 - Defining the Relationship)

**The Conversation:**

Eden concerned about what being with him means. The danger. The command decisions.

**Not about protection** - about mutual understanding and operational protocols.

**Lyren's Clarity:**
- *"I threw myself in front of death burst. I KNOW what it means to care about someone doing dangerous work."*
- *"What I CAN'T handle is you pushing me away to 'protect' me."*

**The Protocols Established:**

**Capture:** Don't trade yourself. Coordinate extraction.

**Mind Control:** Safe word pending (for "not compromised, just weird")

**Death/Injury:** Not at will-writing stage (third date territory). Defer to healers but consent to experimental treatment if dying.

**Operational Security:** General info shareable. Classified: case by case.

**Communication:** Actually tell each other about dangerous things.

**The Second Kiss:**
- After protocols
- Lyren: *"No documentation. We trust each other."*
- Kissed him to stop protocol talk
- Eden: *"Oh, now YOU shut MY mouth."* → pulled her back

**What Changed:**
- Established they're partners, not protector/protected
- Structure through protocols (their love language)
- Trust formalized
